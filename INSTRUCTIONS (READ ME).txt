----INSTRUCTIONS----
 Use the W,A,S,D keys to move the player
 Use the space bar to attack.
 Keep the command prompt visible so that you can see your players current HP.
(HP will be part of the UI in time)

----WARNINGS----
 Try not to get trapped against the walls by the enemies, you will lose health very
 quickly if you get hit against a wall.

----OBJECTIVE----
 Kill all of the enemies to win or die trying.


GOOD LUCK!
